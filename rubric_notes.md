# Strategy Report Rubric - 5-Point Criteria

## 1. Industry Trends (5 pts)
- Explains trend's effects and **quantifiable business implications**
- Teaches customer something new about their own market
- Creates a compelling sense of urgency
- Asks customer if they see application to their business

## 2. Sinch Use Cases (5 pts)
- 4 use cases: **Engaged, Informed, Safe, Happy**
- Seamlessly connect all industry-relevant use cases to customer's specific challenges
- Paints a holistic vision of how Sinch uniquely transforms their business
- Asks if customer sees the same alignment

## 3. Buying Committee (5 pts)
- Deep understanding of entire buying committee
- Motivations, metrics, and pain points by persona (even those not in the room)
- Co-develops intersections, conflicts and way forward with the buying committee

## 4. Real-Life Stories (5 pts)
- Compelling and memorable story about a similar customer
- Focuses on emotional and business impact of before/after
- Story's relevance feels like it's about the current customer
- Asks customer if they see the relevance

## 5. How Sinch Wins (5 pts)
- Articulates Sinch's value as the logical conclusion to the customer's story
- Differentiators are NOT listed but WOVEN IN as the unique solution
- Addresses customer's specific, high-stakes problem
- Asks customer if they see Sinch's differentiation
